Recycling app

In progress
  • Expo
  • React Native
  • TypeScript

This app serves as a guide to properly dispose of waste.

It contains a database of waste items (packages, unused items, food and other waste) and the right place to dispose of them.

All information is sourced from official websites and articles from professionals.

Design
Source

What can it do?

The main tab introduces users with recycling bins and provides a summary of what to throw in each one.

Searching for items

In the search tab users can search for items in the database using keywords.

Next to each item is a clear indication of where such item should be disposed of. Most items have additional information detailing the nuances of recycling.
